Dots and Boxes



How to Play

The game starts with an empty square grid of dots. Starting with player one each player takes turns adding a single horizontal or vertical line to connect unjoined dots. When a player connects the fourth side of a 1x1 box they score one point and take another turn. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins.
